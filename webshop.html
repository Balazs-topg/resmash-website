Our Courts

Welcome to our website! Here at Resmash, we specialize in buying and selling used padel courts throughout Sweden and the rest of the EU. On our website we showcase a selection of the padel courts that we currently have available for purchase.

In addition to the courts on our website, we also have other contacts throughout Sweden that allow us to source a wider variety of used padel courts. If you don't see the exact court you're looking for on our website, don't hesitate to contact us and we'll do our best to find what you need.

At Resmash, we pride ourselves on our ability to deliver high-quality used padel courts to our customers. We work closely with each of our clients to understand their unique needs and preferences, and we're committed to providing a smooth and seamless buying experience.

To learn more about our services and how we can help you find the perfect used padel court, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're always happy to answer any questions you may have and assist you in any way we can.
