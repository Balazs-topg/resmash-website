Welcome to our company! We're glad to have you with us.

We are a team of experts in the field of padel courts, specializing in buying and selling used courts across Sweden and the EU. Our mission is to provide high-quality courts to our customers while ensuring a hassle-free and seamless experience.

Our team has a wealth of knowledge and experience in this industry, which allows us to source and deliver courts that meet our customers' unique needs and requirements. We are dedicated to ensuring customer satisfaction, and we go the extra mile to make sure that each customer receives the best possible experience.

We take great pride in the quality of our courts, and we work tirelessly to ensure that they meet our high standards. We only deal with courts that have been well-maintained, ensuring that they are in excellent condition for our customers.

Our team is always on hand to answer any questions or concerns you may have, and we will guide you through every step of the process, from selecting the right court to delivery and installation. We take care of all the details, so you can sit back, relax, and enjoy your new court.

Thank you for choosing us, and we look forward to serving you!

1. Contact: The customer contacts us with a request to buy a used padel court.

2. Consultation: We consult with the customer to understand their needs and preferences for the padel court they want to buy.

3. Search: We use our extensive network and resources to search for a suitable used padel court that matches the customer's requirements.

4. Inspection: Once we have found a suitable used padel court, we inspect it thoroughly to ensure that it meets our high-quality standards.

5. Delivery: We coordinate the delivery of the padel court to the customer's location.

6. Installation: If requested, we can also arrange for the installation of the padel court at the customer's site.

7. Follow-up: After the delivery and/or installation, we follow up with the customer to ensure that everything has been done to their satisfaction.

After we have found a suitable court and inspected it to ensure it meets our customer's expectations, we will request a reservation fee from the customer. This fee secures the purchase and covers any expenses related to dismantling, transportation, and reinstallation of the court. The remaining payment is due upon delivery of the court to the customer. Our goal is to provide a hassle-free and seamless process for our customers, from start to finish.
